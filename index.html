About American Corner Mtaani

A vibrant U.S.-funded community hub at Kenya National Library Service (KNLS) Nairobi – Upper Hill

American Corner Mtaani is your local gateway to American resources, education, culture, and opportunities. Located inside the Kenya National Library Service (KNLS) Maktaba Kuu Building on Ngong Road, Upper Hill, Nairobi, it is one of several American Spaces across Kenya.

Fully funded by the American people through the U.S. Department of State and the U.S. Embassy Nairobi, we provide free access to information, technology, and programs that empower Kenyans to connect, create, and collaborate.

U.S.-Kenya Partnership

Bridging cultures and creating opportunities since the American Spaces program began.

Technology & Innovation

Modern tech tools, maker spaces, and digital resources for the 21st century.

Education & Skills

Free programs on entrepreneurship, leadership, English, career development & more.

Whether you’re a student, entrepreneur, researcher, teacher, or community leader — American Corner Mtaani is your space to learn, grow, and connect with the world.
