About American Corner Mtaani

U.S.-funded hub at KNLS Nairobi

American Corner Mtaani is a vibrant American Space located at Kenya National Library Service (KNLS) Upper Hill. Fully funded by the U.S. Embassy Nairobi, we provide free access to technology, education, and cultural exchange programs.

From entrepreneurship training to 3D printing and podcasting, we empower Kenyan youth, students, and entrepreneurs.
